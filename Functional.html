Functional Modification

Functional Car Modification is the maintainence of the functionality of the cars. Like Air Conditioning, Parking Sense etc.functional modifications are those which change or add certain functions to the vehicle.Installation of sunroofs,air conditioning, car phone,roof rack,navigation system,etc.comeunder this category.Functionalities added to a vehicle which were notthere earlier are calledfunctional mods.

Types of functional modifications:

There are variety of modifications in the functional modifications of cars that are given below:

1.Satellite navigation system:

A satellite navigation system is a very useful upgrade if you do not already have it in your car. Most automobile manufacturers offer this feature in their cars these days. A navigation system uses Global Positioning System (GPS) to chalk out the best route from point A to point B. It provides information regarding the traffic conditions and thus saves time and makes the journey as hasslefree as possible.

2.Air conditioning:

Car air conditioning is a system within your car that allows you to cool the interior air of the vehicle in hot weather, providing for a cooler environment for the occupants. Refrigerant changes from gas to liquid, and back to gas, and is a vital part of the air conditioning system and process.

3.Car Phone:

The car phone offers the driver, the liberty of using his/her phone while driving. The driver can make or receive phone calls without even touching the phone. It has become even easier to use a car phone these days as most of the modern infotainment systems in cars now offer bluetooth compatibility which helps them connect their phones to the car’s media system directly.

4.Parking sensors:

Parking a car can be a tricky job, especially in tight corners. Parking sensors make the whole easier. Parking sensors can guide the driver while parking by providing information regarding obstacles that are in the way. Many cars offer parking sensors as a standard in-built feature these days. But, if your car doesn’t have one, it is definitely a good modification to be considered.

5.Roof racks:

A roof rack is a perfect carrier that enables you to load up your vehicle and carry bulky equipment from sporting goods to camping gear and a range of other items. It's also important to remember to always secure your load regardless of the type of roof rack or rail installed to your vehicle.

6.Hand controls:

Hand controls allow people with physical disabilities the ability to drive accessible vehicles independentlyusing alternative methods. Hand controls allow the driver to operate both the brake and gas pedal using levers that are typically mounted below the steering wheel and attach to the pedals themselves.



7.Additional headLights:

Additional headlights provide significant additional safety when visibility is poor and in bad weather conditions. The additional light often gives drivers those vital seconds in critical hazardous situations. Upgrading standard vehicle lighting is always to be recommended and can save lives.

8.Installation of sunroofs:

Sunroof allows fresh air in the cabin when driving. It gives freedom and creates sort of a romantic ambience. If you plan to keep the windows open, there is so much noise of the gushing wind which makes it too noisy even to converse with the fellow passengers. The sunroof is very good in such situations.
